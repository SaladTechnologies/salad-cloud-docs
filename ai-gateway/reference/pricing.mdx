## Closed Beta

During the closed beta period, Salad AI Gateway is available at a flat rate of **$20/month**, regardless of usage
volume. This covers access to all available models.

[Sign up for early access](https://salad.com/ai-gateway)

## Per-Token Pricing

The following per-token rates apply and will be used for billing during beta period and once the service moves beyond
the flat-rate beta.

| Model             | Input ($/M tokens) | Output ($/M tokens) |
| :---------------- | -----------------: | ------------------: |
| `qwen3.5-35b-a3b` |              $0.08 |               $0.65 |
| `qwen3.5-27b`     |              $0.10 |               $0.80 |
| `qwen3.5-9b`      |              $0.03 |               $0.08 |

Prices are per million tokens. Input tokens include your prompt and any conversation history sent with each request.
Output tokens are the generated response.
